About

I am currently pursuing an M.Sc. in Mechanical Engineering at Technion under Prof. René van Hout and Assoc. Prof. Cheng Li. My work centers on multiphase flows, bubble bursting physics, turbulence, and advanced optical diagnostics including Tomographic PIV and Digital In-line Holography. Alongside research, I have experience as a teaching assistant and research assistant, with earlier project leadership in robotics, logistics systems, and product-mechanical innovation.
