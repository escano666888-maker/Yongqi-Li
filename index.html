多相流、气泡破裂与先进成像研究作品集 Research Portfolio in Multiphase Flow, Bubble Bursting, and Advanced Imaging

目前就读于以色列理工学院机械工程硕士，研究方向为实验流体力学、多相流以及光学诊断技术。当前研究集中于湍流诱导液滴破碎的先进成像技术开发，以及寿命驱动的稀释水包油乳浊液气泡多孔破裂与液滴生成机理研究。同时具有机器人设计、物流分拣、协同搬运、增材制造系统设计与本科科研创新项目经历。

I am an M.Sc. student in Mechanical Engineering at Technion, working in experimental fluid mechanics, multiphase flow, and optical diagnostics. My current research focuses on advanced imaging of turbulence-induced droplet fragmentation and on lifetime-driven multihole bursting and droplet generation in dilute oil-in-water emulsion bubbles. I also have prior experience in robotics design, logistics sorting systems, collaborative transport platforms, and additive manufacturing projects.
